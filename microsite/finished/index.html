About Jeremy Jones

Jeremy Jones is a snowboarder who focuses on big mountain riding and climbing. He has been the focus of many snowboarding films, showing off his technical ability and style while highlighting his minimalist approach to the sport. From his career riding, he has gotten involved in snowboard building and environmental activism.

Entrepeneur

Jones Snowboards

Jones Snowboards was founded in 2010 by Jeremy Jones. Based out of Lake Tahoe, they create boards, bindings, and other snowboarding accessories. Jones snowboards largely promotes two causes: avalanche safety and sustainability. Along these lines, they are a member of 1% For The Planet, donating 1% of sales to environmental causes.
